Before You Book

Questions Every First-Time Client Asks

Who analyses my chart — a human or software?

Every paid consultation is personally conducted live, 1-on-1 over video. The free AI assistant on this page answers quick questions; your full reading is never software-generated.

Is my personal information kept private?

Completely. Your birth details, questions and session recordings are never shared with anyone. All consultations are strictly confidential.

What if I'm not satisfied with the consultation?

If you feel your core question wasn't addressed, message us within 7 days and we'll schedule a follow-up session at no charge. Your trust matters more than any single booking.

How accurate are the predictions?

Accuracy depends on a precise birth time and place. With correct details, Vedic analysis can be remarkably specific about timing windows — over 1,200 clients have found the readings actionable.

How long until I receive my report?

The PDF report for video consultations arrives within 48 hours of your session. Email-only numerology and palm reports are delivered within 24–48 hours of payment.

What do I need before the session?

Your date, time and place of birth (as exact as possible), and 1–3 questions you most want answered. If your birth time is uncertain, mention it — rectification can be discussed.
