Your trust is sacred to us. This policy explains, in plain language, what information Veshannastro collects when you visit our website or book a consultation, how we use it, and the rights you hold over it.

Who We Are

Veshannastro ("we", "us", "our") provides personalised Vedic astrology and numerology consultation services online, operating from India and serving clients in India and internationally. This Privacy Policy applies to the website veshannastro.co.in and all related booking, consultation, and communication channels.

By using our website or booking a consultation, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

02 · Information We Collect

Information We Collect

To deliver a personalised reading, we collect only what is necessary:

  • Identity & contact details — your name, email address, and phone or WhatsApp number.

We use the information we collect to prepare and deliver your consultation and reports, schedule and confirm your sessions, respond to your enquiries, issue invoices, process payments, send you your deliverables, improve our website and services, and meet our legal and regulatory obligations.

We will only send you promotional communication if you have opted in, and you may withdraw consent at any time.

Payment Processing

Payments are handled by trusted third-party payment processors (such as Razorpay and PayPal). When you make a payment, your financial details are submitted directly to these processors under their own secure, PCI-DSS-compliant systems.
